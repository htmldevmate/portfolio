EVERYTHING YOU WANT

With 40 years of experience, Mountain Air is your Base Camp, situated a 100 metres from Medran main chairlift and in the heart of Place Blanche. We are world-renowned for our boot fitting service and have an incredible selection of skis to try, buy or rent – including alpine, backcountry, touring and telemark – as well as the largest collection of technical and fashion clothing in Verbier. Our expert and knowledgeable staff are passionate about the mountains and are on-hand to make sure you enjoy them to the fullest.

Mountain Air is more than just a shop. We are your all in one mountain hub with all the gear you could dream of, our very own Brasserie Le Bec where you can refuel and relax, have a chat with our specialists, learn the skills you’ve been looking for with our partners at European Snowsport, shop clothes in our lifestyle section, buy ski passes, leave your skis overnight while you go off to après-ski, rent sleighs, pick up new goggles… anything and everything you want.

We are on-hand so you can start and finish your ski day in comfort at Mountain Air.
